# FSP Parts — Agent Context

## Design Context

- **Register:** product (e-commerce catalog for HVAC/R parts — design serves the workflow)
- **Users:** B2B professionals — HVAC/R technicians and contractors in Colombia
- **Full design brief:** see `PRODUCT.md` (strategic) and `DESIGN.md` (visual system)

### Five design principles (from PRODUCT.md)
1. **Spec over spectacle.** The catalog is the product. Hierarchy, density, and scannability over flair.
2. **Earn trust through precision.** A sloppy interface signals a sloppy supply chain.
3. **Density without noise.** Purposeful density — no padding inflation to fill space.
4. **Workflow over persuasion.** This is a tool, not a storefront. No discount language or urgency patterns.
5. **Technical vocabulary as first-class content.** SKUs, part numbers, and refrigerant codes are not metadata — they are the user's primary language.
